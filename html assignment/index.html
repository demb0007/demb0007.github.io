About me

My name is Amel, and I am currently a student studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. Before continuing my studies, I worked as an English teacher, which helped me develop strong communication and presentation skills. I also own a serigraphy workshop where I work with creative production tools, including two CNC machines: a CNC laser cut machine and a CNC router. This experience allowed me to combine creativity, technology, and hands-on problem solving.

Outside of school, I like exploring new technologies, working on personal projects, and improving my problem-solving skills. I am currently in my 1st year and I am excited to continue learning more about design and development. I enjoy challenges because they help me grow both academically and personally.
